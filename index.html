My Basketball Journey

My name is Julian Hernandez and basically my Basketball journey started 9 years ago. When we got to the park i thought he was taking so i can go play on the slides and the swings but i was wrong he took me and told me he was going to start teaching me how to play basketball. When i got Home i started telling my mom how i wanted to start playing for a team and one day when i was in school they gave me a paper to like tryout for the USA youth sports and i joined and ever since then i started getting better and better. I won like 4 trophies with The Youth sports team and the coaches that i had were awesome they taught me so much and as i got older i started getting better until one day i went to tryouts for middle school and made it and starting practing even more.
